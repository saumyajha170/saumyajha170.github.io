SERVICES

Kalyan Seva – an KalyanSeva is a home away from home for the widows, where they are not just looked after but taken care of as our own. We provide accommodation, nursing care, food, and various other facilities to the residents on a non-profit basis. We also provide a home for the senior citizens, where they are taken care of as our own family and also provide various facilities.

Accommodation, Food, Clothing

Accommodation is provided to the registered woman. A team of caregivers help in feeding and clothing of the elderly who can’t help themselves.

24 x 7 Nursing Care

The elderly are provided with proper medication and treatment as per the diagnosis made by the team of doctors. Medical team and trained nursing staff are around the elderly 24*7.

Daily Activities

It includes laughter clubs, exercises/yoga, temple visits, medication. The elderly love listening to music and watching TV. There are two television systems that are installed for their entertainment.

About Us

Hi! This is a donation website for an old age home VridhaAshram. An old age home is a shelter that is home to the older and needy people. The steady security in an old age home gives them protection from intruders and helps them live a safe and secure life. One of the factors that make old age homes attractive to elders is the companionship. They are in constant company of people of their own age. To maintain their company and security we are here with this donation site where a single contribution can make a big difference. Your one click can bring smile on their faces.
